Drunken Cockles Recipe

Description

My favourite dish that originated from Putien, China. This is a recipe for drunken spicy cockles.

Ingredients

  • 400g cockles
  • 2 red chilli (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1.5 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1.5 tbsp shaoxing wine
  • 3/4 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 pinches of sugar
  • Some salt

Steps

  1. Scrub cockles clean.
  2. Soak cockles in heavily salted cold water, for 1 hour or more. This would allow the cockles to release dirt. Change water from time to time if it gets too dirty
  3. Prepare sauce while cockles are being soaked.
  4. Heat up pan and add cooking oil. Add garlic and chilli. Then off fire.
  5. Add shaoxing wine, light soy sauce, sugar, and salt. Stir, and set aside to cool.
  6. Boil cockles for 5 seconds.
  7. Drain cockles, and place in cold water.
  8. Remove half shell.
  9. Add sauce, and chopped red chilli.
