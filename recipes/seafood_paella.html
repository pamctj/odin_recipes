Seafood Paella Recipe

Description

Fell in love with this dish when I was travelled to Spain for the first time in 2014. I recall fondly having a 5 euro paella and a coke at a roadside stall.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups chicken stock
  • 1/4 tsp saffron
  • Pinch of sale
  • 1.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 yellow onion
  • 1/4 red bell pepper
  • 1.5 cloves garlic
  • 85g chorizo
  • 200g tomatoes
  • 1.5 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 220g shrimp
  • 220g mussels
  • 220 clams
  • 1/8 cup parsley

Steps

  1. Bring stock to a boil. Turn off heat, steep saffron for at least 15 mins.
  2. Heat oil. Add onion and bell pepper, cook 5-7 mins until onion is transparent.
  3. Stir in garlic and chorizo.
  4. Add uncooked rice and cook, stirring often, for 4-5 mins until rice is lightly toasted.
  5. Stir in the stock, tomatoes, and peas.
  6. Spread the rice evenly.
  7. Close and cook without stirring for 15 mins, or until the rice absorbs most of the stock.
  8. Add seafood, cook for 10 more minutes.
  9. Open cover and cook.
  10. Sprinkle parsley.
